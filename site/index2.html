Our Mission

SHUDDHI is a registered organisation working in India and Globally to help people and NGOs raise funds for Social Causes.Shuddhi Global is an Online Platform developed for People and Non Profits to Raise Funds, Shop GoGreen Products, Organise Events, Share Success Stories etc.

Saving Children

Our main mission is to save and rescue permanently displaced children.

Peace On The Planet

By working with our partners, we aim to establish peaceful relationships.

Care & Protection

We provide global care and protection to support children all over the world.

Causes that need your concern

There’s no place for hunger: access to good quality food for all is possible, and essential for a thriving society. Everyone should have access to good food. No one should have to go to bed hungry. At SHUDDHI, we exist to make giving go further, so together we can transform more lives and communities around the world.
