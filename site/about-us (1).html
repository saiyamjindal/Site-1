SHUDDHI NGO

SHUDDHI is a registered organisation working in India and Globally to help people and NGOs raise funds for Social Causes.

This is the online fundraising software for empowering nonprofits, people, and the causes they believe in. Shuddhi is a team of motivated and professional individuals who share the same dream of making our world a better place to live in and make a difference that last for coming generations and benefit millions of people living around the world.

Shuddhi Global has changed lives of millions of people around the globe in the past years. People and communities have benefitted by using this platform and the number of beneficiaries increase every year.

Our Mission

Achieving Change

The art of life lies in a constant readjustment to our surroundings.

Care & Protection

The ache for home lives in all of us. The safe place where we can go as we are and not be questioned.

Saving Children

Family is precious. Keeping family members healthy, safe, and well provided-for is a cause to which we can all rally.

Peace On The Planet

Every breath we take, every step we make, can be filled with peace, joy and serenity.

Increasing Awareness

Awareness is empowering.. Awareness is observation without choice, condemnation, or justification.

Sharing Happiness

The surest way to be happy is to seek happiness for others.
